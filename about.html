About

Hi, my name is Andres (most people just call me Andy) and I'm a Brooklyn based artist and blogger. I'm mostly self-taught, but I've taken classes at the Art Students League of New York. I'm interested in writing about art, music, Japanese, philosophy, technology, coffee, cooking, and anything fun! I work in Hoboken (well, now I only work from home due to COVID).
